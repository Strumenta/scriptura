Conclusion

  • A hybrid recommender combining collaborative factors with content signals delivers the best overall ranking quality.
  • Cold-start is substantially improved by content representations (genres + keywords).
  • Offline metrics (nDCG@10/MAP@10) show consistent gains over baselines.

Recommendations

  • Deploy hybrid scoring with α≈0.6–0.8 tuned per segment.
  • Add temporal decay and session-aware re-ranking for recency.
  • Introduce diversity constraints (MMR or xQuAD) to reduce popularity bias.
  • Build an ANN index for fast candidate retrieval; apply a lightweight re-ranker.
  • Plan online A/B tests to validate offline–online alignment.

Future Work

  • Leverage transformer embeddings (e.g., plot summaries) for richer content features.
  • Context-aware models (time of day, device) and multi-objective optimization (engagement + novelty).
