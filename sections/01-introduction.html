Introduction

This report describes the design and evaluation of a film recommendation system that helps users discover movies they are likely to enjoy. Recommenders are widely used in streaming platforms, dramatically increasing engagement and watch time by surfacing relevant items within large catalogs.

Purpose

Our goal is to build a modular, production-friendly pipeline that can rank films for each user based on historical interactions and content attributes. The system should support cold-start users/titles, scale to millions of events, and produce business-meaningful metrics.

Scope

  • Offline model training and evaluation on a public dataset (e.g., MovieLens).
  • Ranking for Top-K recommendations; optional rating prediction for comparison.
  • Hybrid approach combining collaborative and content-based signals.

Out of Scope

  • UI/UX for a consumer app and A/B testing in production.
  • Hard real-time serving infrastructure (we simulate it offline).
