Discussion

Findings

  • The hybrid approach outperforms individual models on all Top-K metrics.
  • Content features mitigate item cold start; collaborative factors capture subtle taste.
  • Beyond ~128 latent dimensions, gains diminish relative to training time.

Error Analysis

  • Popularity bias: over-recommending blockbusters lowers diversity.
  • Long-tail coverage: niche titles need re-ranking with diversity constraints.
  • Temporal drift: older interactions can misrepresent current taste without decay.

Limitations

  • Sparse feedback; few interactions for many users.
  • No causal evaluation; offline metrics may not correlate perfectly with CTR.
  • Limited item metadata (no full text/embeddings for trailers or posters).

Ethics & Responsible AI

  • Avoid filter bubbles by promoting calibrated diversity and serendipity.
  • Ensure transparency (why was this recommended?).
  • Respect privacy; use anonymized, minimally necessary data.
